Palani De Praiso: a place you'd love to be

Have you ever been to palani?
Where white sand and clear seas.
Are to true to be seen,
My facorite place, my comfort place.

Sunset falls, sunshine rises,
A beautiful scenery, for the people to see.
The sound of the waves, the sound of the sand,
The sound of nature, that we love to be.

The cold breeze coming from the sea,
The hotness coming from the sun.
The wind dancing, the trees waving,
Join with us, to have fun in the sun.

Hop into the kayak, to explore the seas,
People playing volleyball, the children running around.
Travel to Masbate, Drive to Palani,
Experience the calmness of the sea.

A Glimpse of Him

With the same height as me, yet he stands so tall.
Brown eyes that look with warmth.
I can't deny the fact-that i melt,
Whenever we hold eye contact.

Handsome, yet humble, a heart that is true,
In every glance, he's something new.
He makes my heart flutter,
My stomach filled with butterflies.

He's not like other boys,
he's a gentleman, with manners so pure.
Treates me like I'm the only girl in the world,
He filled the hole in my heart.

His words that makes me feel secure,
His actions that fill my heart with joy.
His soft smile that lightens up my mood,
A queit presence, yet so important to me.

In him, I see perfection,
Cuteness that sneaks in with every move.
A charm that settles, gentle and smooth,
The boy he is, a part of my hearth silently.

"Deeply in love, I become so blind"

Something lingers within my mind,
I go back in time where we we'd meet each other.
Oh the times we we'd to play together,
You're so nice to me-it feels unreal.

Actions that speak louder than words,
You're being so gentle with care.
Oh I think I've fallen for you too hard,
I broke my parents rule just for you.

Though my mouth they cannot speak,
My heart and mind they truly seek.
As I seek love, I sought within my own heart and mind,
With true love that's hard to find.

Was it all casual?
The times we've been together.
The rollercoasters we've been through,
The memories we shared.

The way you stare at me,
The way you stare at her.
They're both the same,
Yet, you still say you only love me.

Roller coasters are scary at first,
But once you ride it and buckle up.
You can't go back.
All you can do is scream.

As time goes by. your fear eventually fades away,
But not all, not all can recover from a bad one.
The cut that didn't even bleed,
The wound that never heals.

Genre: Children’s Fiction, Comedy, Fantasy
