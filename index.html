Group Poem

The pressure’s on again, it drowns my fragile mind, I chase the goals ahead, yet peace I cannot find, Each sleepless night reminds the hope I left behind, A heavy weight that fate and studies intertwined.

I walk in silence, burdened by expectations, Each task a chain that fuels my frustrations, My mind grows weary from endless calculations, A heart held hostage by harsh examinations.

The pages overflow with lessons hard to take, Each test a heavy storm, a burden I can’t shake, I hide my weary tears though every night they break, This pressure cages me, yet dreams are still at stake
