Buntod: The Place You'd Love To Be

Beneath The water so crystal clear, The reef below felt near. The perfect warm breeze, Always makes my heart at peace.

Everyone laugh along the shore, In buntod's charm, we ask no more. May hearts unite to guard this land, This beatiful gem made by nature's hand

Here, times slows down, the souls find rest- In buntod's heart, we know we're blessed. Buntod lives in our hears that cam and calls the ocean-lovers home.

A Piece Of Me That Still Waits For You

In this friendship of ours, there's this romantic feelings, A silent heartbeat full of waiting. For yet I know I was bound to fall, One look at your eyes, I see it all.

Im here at my bed wondering, Am i really sure to acknowledge this feeling? Some say "waiting drives you crazy" But this affection of mine is strong. never lazy

I always see your face in the crowd, Hear your Chuckle, soft, yet so loud. For in this yearning, I still feel, This love we will never share, felt so real
