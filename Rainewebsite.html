Rafaela Resort: The place you'd love to be

Rafaela Resort has the perfect sunset view,
It captures your heart like a dream that is coming true.
The sunset so clear, it can bring you a gentle tear,
It can make u amaze, that your eyes will start to gaze.

The pool's blue glow creates a peaceful day,
Water so clear, it shines in every way.
The waves never fail to brighten my night,
Soft winds around be makes everything feel right.

Their comfy rooms bring peace into my heart.
No noise or trouble, just calm in each part.
It feels like home, with beauty all around,
At Rafaela, joy is always around.

Charlie Fleming: A person i idolize

She glows, she shines and is kind,
By her beauty, you'll turn blind.
She's kind to all, she cares alot,
She teaches me to love my spot.

Her eyes are soft, they shine like stars,
Her voice can reach both near and far.
She helps me dream and stand up tall,
She shows that i can have it all.

I look at her, i feel so proud,
She is a gem among the crowd.
She is a light i want to be,
A better me, because of she.

The Last Umbrella

The rain poured down, the sky was gray,
But still he ran to where she lay.
He gave his coat to keep her warm,
And braved alone the heavy storm.

The road was long, his feet were sore,
Yet love still pushed him on once more.
He reached her door, then thought with cheer,
"Her smile was worth each step right here."

"When I first saw you

I didn't notice you at first,
How could I when I was at worst.
I didn't care nor know you near,
A random guy just passing here.

But then you caught my eyes that day,
The world around me slowed its way.
Whenever you were near,
People started to disappear.

And though no words were ever shaped,
It felt like somehow, you still cared.
For in those eyes, I found my chance.
A love that bloomed from just a glance.

But i can't find you anymore,
And my tears suddenly pour.
I hope our paths cross again,
Beyond the heartbreak and the pain.
