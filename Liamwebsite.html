Winvel's a place you need to be

In Masbate, where memories resides,
Winvel's stand proud with nothing to hide.
My mothers domain, a sweet creations,
Carrying on family's dedication.

Grandma and Grandpa, the founders so true,
Their legendary cakes, forever in view.
Ube's rich purple, a vibrant delight,
Lecge flan's creamy, a heavenly sight.

The signature ensaymada, golden and bright,
A taste of tradition, pure and light.
Pandesal's crisp crust, a morning's embrace,
Winvel's sweet treats, leave a smile on each face.

From generations past, the recipes flow,
Each baked a good a story, a heart warming show.
Winvel's a landmark, a sweet cherished place,
A legacy of love, time cannot erase.

Difference between Modern Filipino Games and Traditional games

Filipino Games, or Larong Pinoy, Reflect the Culture, Creativity, and values of Filipino Communities. Traditional Filipino Games Such as Tumbang preso, Patintero, and luksong Baka can be compared and contrasted with modern games. Especially digital and online games, in terms of purpose, interaction, and culture

Traditional Filipino games are usually played outdoors and require physical activity, teamwork, and face-to-face interactions. For example, Patintero encourages cooperation between each team and strategize, among players while Tumbang Preso Develops agility and quick thinking and reaction. These games often use simple materials like slippers, chalk, or cans, showing resourcefulness and simplicity. In contrast, to modern games are mostly played indoors and using different gadgets like, cellphone, laptop, and gaming consoles. They focus more on virtual interaction, advanced graphics, and individual achievement rather than physical touch.

Despite their differences, both traditional and modern provide entertainment and help relieve stress. They also develop skills such as solving problems, competitiveness, and discipline. However, larong pinoy strongly promotes Filipino Values Like pakikisama, respect, and community bonding, which are less shown on online games

In conclusion, modern games are for convenience and technological excitement, Traditional Filipino games preserve culture, identity, and social connection, balancing both of these types of games can help younger generations to learn and improve their skills while still appreciating the Filipino heritage
