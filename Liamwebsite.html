Winvel's a place you need to be

In Masbate, where memories resides, Winvel's stand proud with nothing to hide. My mothers domain, a sweet creations, Carrying on family's dedication.

Grandma and Grandpa, the founders so true, Their legendary cakes, forever in view. Ube's rich purple, a vibrant delight, Lecge flan's creamy, a heavenly sight.

The signature ensaymada, golden and bright, A taste of tradition, pure and light. Pandesal's crisp crust, a morning's embrace, Winvel's sweet treats, leave a smile on each face.

From generations past, the recipes flow, Each baked a good a story, a heart warming show. Winvel's a landmark, a sweet cherished place, A legacy of love, time cannot erase.

Iron man and Me

From childhood days to teenage years, you taught me to be brave, To do what's right, to help the weak, and choose the lives we save. In trials dark, you showed me how to trust and stand tall, Iron man, you lit the way - your courage inspires us all.

A Canvas of Me

I dive in the water, so calm and crystal blue, Each stroke i take feels refreshing, pure and true. The waves are my refuge. they teach me to renew, In swimming I find love to chase the dreams I view.

In colors of art I express what's deep inside, The canva reveals where my secret thoughts reside. With brushes and shades all my vision come alive, Through painting I learn how my passin will survive

In sculpting I mold form with my patient hands, Form clay into beauty, heart soon understands. My passions define, me like waves upon the sand, A soul ever searching, yet firm enough to stand.
